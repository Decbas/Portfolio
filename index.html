Planning

In this project, the plan to design and create a gorilla. The gorilla will be based on an action figure that will be scan and print using various resources available. Various tools will be used such as 3D scanning, Blender for editing. To enhance the figure's mobility, the plan is to incorporate circular ball joints into the arms. The concept includes adding fur to parts of the gorilla that typically have hair on a real gorilla, simulating the animal's appearance authentically. They intend to dye the gorilla's hair black while leaving the chest hairless to simulate natural characteristics. During the development stage, the team will employ a 3D printer to produce their project in separate pieces. They plan to print two arms and the remainder of the body as one unit. The material that will be used is PLA (Polylactic Acid) for printing. The reason for this choice is that it is good for 3D scanning and is biodegradable, which is environmentally friendly, as it is made from non-finite resources such as corn starch and sugar cain.
